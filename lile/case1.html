REC VID

We received funds from NAYCOM for the purpose of sensitising youth to enhance peace and non-violence during the June 24 general elections of Sierra Leone. In that light, two major activities were implemented which include sensitisation workshop and radio discussion. The sensitisation workshop was geared towards educating youth to desist from violent actions that may hinder the electoral process. Therefore, the participants agreed to adopt a benign approach to the election while acting as peace ambassadors. At the same time, we had a radio discussion program in which the public was sensitised by disseminating peace messages and denouncing hate speech, threat and intimidation against political opponents. Many people participated in the radio phone-in-event which contributed to the maintenance of peaceful election throughout Tonkolili district.
